Vision

To turn the hearts of man back to God and to disciple them to reach the full stature of Christ. (Zech 1: 3-6, Matt 28: 19-20 Eph 4: 11-16)

Mission

To attract and then develop members into mature models of Christ like character and conduct, mobilized for ministry (in the church) and a meaningful mission (in the world) in each stage and segment of their lives.

Our Target Market

  • THE COMMUNITY - uncommitted

  • THE CROWD – committed to attending

  • THE CONGREGATION - committed to membership

  • THE COMMITTED - committed to maturity

  • THE CORE – committed to ministry and mission

  • Send them out

Theological Foundations

  1. Christianity is a LIFESTYLE!

    I have come that you might have LIFE, and have it more abundantly! -Jesus Christ,(John 10:10).

  2. This LIFESTYLE has 4 key components

    • Every believer is a member of Christ’s Body. (Rom. 12:5)

    • Every believer needs maturity. (Eph. 4:13)

    • Every believer is a minister. (Eph. 4:12)

    • Every believer is a missionary. (Acts 1:8)

  3. These components meet four basic life needs

    • People to live with

    • Principles to live by

    • A profession to live on

    • A purpose to live for
